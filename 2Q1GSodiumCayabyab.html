Content Section

The Chronicles of Narnia is book series by C.S. Lewis. The book series has seven books in total.
The book contains allegories of Christianity, with Aslan representing Jesus Christ.
There are also many Christian allegories in the boom such as: Aslan sacrificing himself for Edmund,
the ressurection of Aslan, the silver apple, and the New Narnia at the end of the book.

Here are the books in order of release:
1) The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe
2) Prince Caspian
3) The Voyage of the Dawn Treader
4) The Silver Chair
5) The Horse and His Boy
6) The Magician's Nephew
7) The Last battle

I read the compiled version of the Chronicles of Narnia. I was able to finish the book in a few weeks because I genuinely liked the story and wanted to know what happened next.
However, it is quite wordy so I wouldn't recommend it to anyone with a small attention span.
